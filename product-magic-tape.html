Ideal for Rentals, Apartments, and Pet‑Friendly Homes

The MBORMC® Magic Tape Fly Screen was designed with two key users in mind: renters who cannot drill into window frames, and cat owners whose furry friends love to scratch traditional mesh screens. Our heavy‑duty polyester fiber mesh is specially woven to resist snagging and tearing – even when a curious cat paws at it repeatedly. The white border fabric creates a neat, finished look that matches most interior windows.

Simple peel‑and‑stick installation: The system includes two strips of high‑bond hook‑and‑loop tape (one side adheres to the window frame, the other is sewn onto the screen). Clean the frame, peel off the backing, press the tape onto the frame, then attach the screen. That's it – no measuring mistakes, no tools, no permanent changes. The screen can be removed and re‑attached dozens of times without losing adhesion.

Why choose polyester over fibreglass? Polyester mesh is softer, more flexible, and less prone to fraying than fibreglass. It also resists UV degradation better and washes easily with mild soap. Our mesh density (18×16 threads per inch) is fine enough to block mosquitoes, midges, and even sand flies while maintaining over 80% airflow and clear outward vision.

Custom made for your exact window: Each screen is manufactured at our MBORMC facility in Foshan, China according to the width and height you provide. We cut the mesh and sew the Velcro border precisely to your dimensions – no DIY trimming required (though you can trim the frame tape if needed). The finished product is folded and packed in a carton box for safe international shipping by sea or air freight.

Applications: Perfect for outward‑opening windows that cannot accommodate rigid frames, sliding windows, casement windows, and even small bathroom vents. The white border with grey mesh complements both light and dark window trims, maintaining a clean, minimalist aesthetic.
