Perfect for Bedrooms, Living Rooms & Home Offices – No Drills, No Cords

The MBORMC® Cordless Blackout Cellular Shade is engineered for modern homes where safety, simplicity, and style matter. Unlike traditional blinds with dangling cords that pose strangulation risks to children and pets, our cordless design uses a spring‑assisted mechanism – simply lift or lower the bottom rail by hand, and the shade stays exactly where you place it.

True no‑drill installation: We offer several zero‑damage mounting methods. For tension‑mount shades, the shade fits snugly inside the window frame using spring pressure – no screws, no adhesives, no tools. For adhesive‑mount versions, we provide industrial‑grade double‑sided tape that bonds securely to clean surfaces and removes cleanly later. If you prefer permanent mounting, inside/outside/top mount brackets can be screwed in (drill required, but we also supply adhesive pads as alternative).

Insulating honeycomb design: The cellular structure creates air pockets that act as a thermal barrier. This helps keep your room cooler in summer (reducing air conditioning costs) and warmer in winter (cutting heating bills). The fabric is also waterproof and moisture‑resistant, so it won't warp or grow mould in humid environments like kitchens, bathrooms, or basements.

Customised to your exact window: We manufacture each shade based on the width and height you provide. Available for any window type – UK sash windows, US double‑hung or sliding windows, Australian casement and awning windows, and outward‑opening windows commonly found in these regions. The shade can be mounted inside the frame (flush fit), outside the frame (overlap), or at the top of the window (top mount).

Materials & packaging: The shade is made from woven polyester fabric, available in a range of colours (white, beige, grey, black, and custom). All components are lead‑free and child‑safe. Each order is packed in a custom‑sized carton box to ensure safe transit from our MBORMC factory in Foshan, China to your doorstep. We ship worldwide by sea or air freight.
