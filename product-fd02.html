Built for Large Openings: Villas, Patios, Entry Doors

The MBORMC® FD05 is our flagship folding screen door system, specifically engineered for expansive openings up to 4,000mm wide and 2,800mm high. Whether you need a single‑leaf sliding screen for a narrow door or a bi‑parting (French door) configuration for double entrances, the FD05 delivers robust mosquito and fly protection without compromising on aesthetics.

The frame is constructed from premium 6063‑T5 aluminium, available in three standard colours – white, grey, or realistic wood grain – to match your existing door or window trims. Unlike cheap PVC alternatives, aluminium remains rigid, resistant to warping, and easy to clean.

The core of the FD05 is its 304 stainless steel concertina mesh. This foldable mesh opens smoothly left or right (or both sides for bi‑parting) and compresses into a compact stack when not in use. Stainless steel provides exceptional resistance against rust, pet scratching, and accidental impact – ideal for homes with children or pets.

Installation notes: The FD05 is mounted directly into your door frame using a clamping system. For a perfect result, you will need a spirit level and electric drill to fix the guide rails and housing. While not a purely “tool‑free” product, the process is straightforward and can be completed by a confident DIYer or a local handyman. We provide detailed instructions and video support.

All FD05 screens are made to order at our MBORMC factory in Dali Town, Nanhai, Foshan, China. After production, each unit is securely packed in a wooden crate and shipped via sea freight (or air freight for urgent orders). We serve customers worldwide – from Australia and New Zealand to the UK, USA, Canada, and Europe.
