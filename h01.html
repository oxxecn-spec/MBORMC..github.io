Product Details & Specifications

MBORMC® H01 is the ultimate DIY retractable fly screen for homeowners across the United Kingdom, United States, and Australia. Unlike off‑the‑shelf screens, the H01 is 100% custom manufactured at our Foshan factory based on your window measurements – guaranteeing a perfect fit for any window type, including UK sash windows, US sliding windows, Australian casement windows, as well as inward‑opening and outward‑opening windows.

The frame is crafted from premium 6063‑T5 aluminium alloy, available in any RAL colour to match your existing window frames. The screen uses a high‑density PET fiber mesh (and an optional 304 stainless steel mesh layer for extra resistance against pets or harsh conditions), providing excellent insect protection while maintaining over 85% airflow and clear outward visibility.

Installation is truly tool‑free: the H01 comes with a high‑strength double‑sided adhesive tape that bonds securely to aluminium, PVC, wood, or metal frames. Simply clean the surface, peel, and stick – no drills, no screws, no professional help. Men, women, and the elderly can complete the installation in under 15 minutes.

From our factory in Dali Town, Nanhai, Foshan, to your doorstep – we make custom insect protection accessible anywhere.

Whether you need protection against mosquitoes, flies, wasps, midges, or even butterflies, the H01 retractable roll‑up screen rolls away when not in use, keeping your view unobstructed. It is ideal for bedrooms, living rooms, kitchens, and patios.
