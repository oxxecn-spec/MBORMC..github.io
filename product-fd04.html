Designed for Active Homes: Kitchens, Garages, Living Rooms & Villa Gates

The MBORMC® FD02 is the ultimate folding door screen for homes with pets, high foot traffic, or demanding environmental conditions. Unlike standard magnetic screens that can be damaged by enthusiastic dogs or curious cats, the FD02 uses a robust 304 stainless steel dot mesh or crystal clear PVC panels that resist scratching, biting, and pushing.

Two panel options for different needs:
• 304 Stainless Steel Dot Mesh: Fine round‑hole mesh that blocks mosquitoes, flies, and midges while providing excellent airflow. The metal construction is virtually indestructible against pet claws and teeth. Ideal for garages, back doors, and outdoor transitions.
• Crystal Clear PVC Folding Panels: Fully transparent, flexible PVC that lets light through while keeping insects and dust out. Perfect for kitchens (grease/smoke barrier), living room partitions, or any area where you want an invisible barrier without obstructing the view.

Ideal applications: The FD02 shines in busy areas: kitchens (ventilate while cooking without flies), garage doors (keep pests out while working on cars), living rooms (create a bug‑free zone while maintaining an open feel), and villa main entrances (withstand daily use from family and pets).

Installation & operation: The FD02 mounts into the door frame using a track system. It folds like an accordion – slide it open to one side (single leaf) or both sides (bi‑parting). A bottom guide rail ensures smooth movement. Installation requires basic tools (spirit level, drill, screwdriver) – we provide detailed instructions and technical support.

Factory direct & global shipping: Each FD02 is custom‑manufactured at our MBORMC facility in Dali Town, Nanhai, Foshan, China based on your provided dimensions. After production, the unit is packed in a wooden crate and shipped via sea freight (or air freight for urgent orders) to destinations worldwide – including the UK, USA, Australia, Canada, and Europe.
