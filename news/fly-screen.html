What Is a Fly Screen?

A fly screen (also known as a fly mesh or insect screen) is a fine mesh barrier designed to prevent flies, wasps, bees, and other airborne pests from entering a building. Unlike chemical repellents or electronic zappers, a fly screen provides a passive, chemical‑free, and 24/7 physical defense. It allows you to keep windows and doors wide open for ventilation without worrying about pests. MBORMC screens are custom‑cut to your exact dimensions, ensuring no gaps where insects can sneak in.

Why You Need a Fly Screen in Your Home

Flies are not just a nuisance; they carry diseases such as salmonella, E. coli, and dysentery. Wasps can sting and cause allergic reactions. Even if you don’t suffer from allergies, the constant buzzing of flies can ruin a peaceful meal or a good night’s sleep. A fly screen eliminates these problems. It’s especially important in kitchens, dining areas, and anywhere food is prepared or consumed. For restaurants and cafes, fly screens are often required by health codes. For homes, they provide peace of mind and a more hygienic environment.

Material Options: PET vs. 304 Stainless Steel

MBORMC uses two primary mesh materials. PET fiber mesh is lightweight, breathable, and affordable. It provides excellent fly protection while maintaining high transparency and airflow. PET is UV‑stabilized and won’t rust. It’s available in grey, dark grey, or black. 304 stainless steel mesh is incredibly strong, pet‑proof, and fire‑resistant. It’s ideal for ground‑floor windows or areas where pets may scratch. Stainless steel also offers higher tear resistance and longevity. Both materials can be matched to any frame colour – white, black, bronze, wood grain, or custom RAL.

DIY Installation – No Professional Required

All MBORMC fly screens are designed for hassle‑free DIY installation. Each order includes a step‑by‑step guide with pictures. If you can measure and use a screwdriver, you can install it yourself.

Frequently Asked Questions About Fly Screens

Q: Can I leave my fly screen installed year‑round?
A: Yes. PET mesh and stainless steel are UV‑resistant and weather‑proof. They will not degrade under sun or rain.

Q: How do I clean the fly screen?
A: Use a soft brush or vacuum with a brush attachment. For deeper cleaning, remove the screen (Velcro or clip systems) and rinse with mild soap and water.

Q: Are your screens pet‑proof?
A: Our 304 stainless steel mesh is highly resistant to scratching and biting. PET mesh is less durable against pets, so we recommend stainless steel for homes with dogs or cats.

Q: Do you offer screens for commercial use (restaurants, cafes)?
