Custom Mosquito Screen – Perfect Fit for Any Window or Door, Any Size, Any Shape

Published on May 3, 2026 | By MBORMC Team
Custom Mosquito Screen Aluminum Mosquito Screen Mosquito Screen Frame Mosquito Screen Window Mosquito Screen Door

No two windows or doors are exactly alike – especially in older homes, custom builds, or unique architectural designs. Off‑the‑shelf mosquito screens often leave gaps or don’t fit at all. That’s why MBORMC specializes in custom mosquito screens made to your exact measurements. Whether you have arched windows, oversized patio doors, unusually shaped skylights, or anything in between, we can manufacture a screen that fits perfectly. In this guide, we’ll explain the custom ordering process, measurement tips, and the many options available for your bespoke insect screen.

Why Choose a Custom Mosquito Screen?

  • Perfect fit – no gaps: Insects can’t squeeze through if the screen is precisely cut to your opening.
  • Non‑standard shapes: Arched, circular, triangular, or trapezoidal windows – we can make screens for any shape.
  • Any size: From tiny bathroom vents to giant picture windows (up to 3m × 3m).
  • Match your décor: Choose frame colour and mesh colour to blend with your existing windows.
  • Select the right mechanism: Fixed, retractable, folding, sliding, or magnetic – we build what you need.

How to Order a Custom Screen from MBORMC

Our ordering process is simple and guided:

  • Step 1: Measure – Use our free measurement guide (PDF or video). We recommend measuring width and height at three points and using the smallest measurement. For non‑rectangular shapes, provide a template or detailed drawing.
  • Step 2: Choose screen type – Fixed frame, retractable (roll‑up), pleated (folding), sliding, or magnetic.
  • Step 3: Choose mesh material – PET fiber (standard) or 304 stainless steel (pet‑proof/heavy‑duty).
  • Step 4: Choose colours – Frame colour (white, grey, black, bronze, custom RAL) and mesh colour (grey, dark grey, black).
  • Step 5: Submit your order – We’ll confirm measurements and provide a CAD drawing for approval before production.
  • Step 6: Production & shipping – Typically 7–15 days for manufacturing, then shipping via sea or air freight.

Measurement Tips for a Perfect Fit

  • Use a steel tape measure for accuracy.
  • Measure the inside of the window frame (the actual opening) for fixed screens.
  • For doors, measure the clear opening width and height.
  • For arched windows: measure the width at the widest point and the height from the top of the arch to the bottom. Provide a photo with a ruler for reference.
  • If you’re unsure, we can arrange a video call to guide you through measuring.

Custom Frame Options

We offer several frame profiles tailored to your application:

  • Standard fixed frame (25mm × 12mm): Most common for residential windows.
  • Heavy‑duty fixed frame (30mm × 15mm): For larger or commercial windows.
  • Roll‑formed frame for sliding screens.
  • Z‑frame with spring clips for tool‑free removal.

Custom Shapes – No Problem

We can manufacture screens for:

  • Arched windows (semi‑circular, gothic, elliptical)
  • Circular and oval windows
  • Triangular and trapezoidal windows
  • Porthole windows (boats, RVs)
  • Custom‑shaped skylights

Please provide a template on paper or a detailed dimensional drawing. We’ll create a CAD model and produce the frame accordingly.

Mesh and Colour Customization

Your custom screen can be tailored aesthetically:

  • Frame colours: White (RAL 9016), off‑white, light grey (RAL 7042), dark grey (RAL 7016), black (RAL 9005), bronze (RAL 8017). We can also match any RAL colour for a small fee.
  • Mesh colours: Grey, dark grey, black. Black mesh offers the best outward visibility (less glare) and is less visible from the outside.
  • Mesh density: Standard 18×16 (blocks mosquitoes and flies) or upgrade to 20×20 (blocks no‑see‑ums).

DIY Installation – We Make It Easy

Even custom screens are designed for easy DIY installation. Each order includes detailed instructions and all necessary hardware (screws, clips, spline, etc.). For fixed frames, you simply place the screen into the opening and secure it with clips or screws. For retractable or folding screens, we provide pre‑assembled cassettes and tracks. Most customers complete installation in under an hour with basic tools (drill, screwdriver, level).

Global Shipping with Wooden Crate Protection

We ship worldwide from our factory in Foshan, China. Custom screens are rigid; each order is packed in a custom‑sized wooden crate to prevent damage. Choose sea freight (economic) or air freight (express). We handle all customs paperwork. Personal orders under duty‑free thresholds (e.g., $800 USD, £135 GBP, €150 EU) generally incur no extra taxes.
