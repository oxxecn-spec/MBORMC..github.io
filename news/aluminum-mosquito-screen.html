Aluminum Mosquito Screen – Strong, Lightweight, and Rust‑Proof Frames

Published on May 3, 2026 | By MBORMC Team
Aluminum Mosquito Screen Custom Mosquito Screen Mosquito Screen Frame Heavy Duty Mosquito Screen Mosquito Screen Window

The frame is just as important as the mesh when it comes to durability and longevity. An aluminum mosquito screen combines a lightweight, rust‑proof aluminum frame with high‑quality mesh to provide years of reliable insect protection. MBORMC uses premium 6063‑T5 aluminum alloy for all our framed screens – the same grade used in architectural windows and doors. In this guide, we’ll explain why aluminum is the superior choice for screen frames, the different frame styles available, and how to order a custom aluminum mosquito screen for your home or business.

Why Choose Aluminum Frames?

  • Rust‑proof: Aluminum does not rust, making it ideal for humid coastal areas.
  • Lightweight: Easy to handle and install compared to steel.
  • Strong: 6063‑T5 aluminum has excellent tensile strength and resists bending.
  • Corrosion‑resistant: Powder‑coated finishes add extra protection.
  • Aesthetic: Can be painted any colour (white, grey, black, bronze, wood grain, etc.) to match your window/door frames.

Frame Styles for Aluminum Mosquito Screens

MBORMC offers several frame profiles depending on your application:

  • Standard fixed frame: A simple rectangular frame with corners joined by pre‑formed corner keys. The mesh is held in place by a rubber spline. This is the most common type for window screens.
  • Roll‑formed frame: A continuous frame with no corners – used for sliding screens that ride on tracks.
  • Heavy‑duty frame: Thicker profile (1.2mm wall thickness) for large screens or commercial applications.
  • Z‑frame (self‑storing): A frame that is slightly larger than the window opening and held in place by spring clips – easy to remove for cleaning.

Mesh Material Options for Aluminum Frames

We pair aluminum frames with your choice of PET fiber mesh (standard, UV‑resistant, 18×16 density) or 304 stainless steel mesh (heavy‑duty, pet‑proof). Both are available in grey, dark grey, or black. The mesh is securely splined into the frame and can be replaced if damaged.

Custom Sizing – Any Window, Any Shape

We manufacture each aluminum mosquito screen to your exact dimensions. Provide width and height in cm or inches, and we cut the frame and mesh to size. We can also produce non‑rectangular shapes (arched, circular, trapezoidal) – just provide a template or detailed drawing. Maximum dimensions: up to 2.5m × 2.5m for a single frame (larger requires reinforcement).

How to Measure for an Aluminum Frame Screen

  • For windows that open inward: measure the inside opening width and height.
  • For windows that open outward: measure the outside frame dimensions.
  • Always measure at three points (top, middle, bottom; left, center, right) and use the smallest measurement.
  • Subtract 2‑3mm from each dimension to allow for easy installation.

If you’re unsure, we provide a free measurement guide and can assist via video call.

DIY Installation – Simple Process

Installing a fixed aluminum frame screen is straightforward:

  • Order your custom screen from MBORMC.
  • Clean the window frame thoroughly.
  • If using clips or brackets, install them per the instructions.
  • Place the screen into the opening. For Z‑frames, simply push them into place – the spring clips hold it securely.
  • For screw‑mount frames, attach using the provided screws (pre‑drill holes in the aluminum frame).

Basic tools: screwdriver, level. Time: 10–20 minutes per screen.

Color Options & Powder Coating

Our aluminum frames are available in standard colours: white, off‑white, grey, dark grey, black, and bronze. We can also match any RAL colour for an additional fee. The powder‑coated finish is UV‑resistant and will not fade or chalk for many years.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will an aluminum frame screen warp in the sun?
A: No. 6063‑T5 aluminum is thermally stable and will not warp under normal temperatures.

Q: Can I replace the mesh on an aluminum frame myself?
A: Yes. You can remove the old spline, replace the mesh, and press new spline into the frame groove. We can supply replacement mesh and spline.

Q: Are aluminum frames safe for coastal areas?
A: Yes, aluminum is naturally corrosion‑resistant. For extra protection, choose a powder‑coated finish.

Q: How do I clean an aluminum frame?
A: Wipe with a damp cloth and mild detergent. Do not use abrasive cleaners.
