Why Your Bedroom Window Needs a Mosquito Screen

Bedrooms are where we rest and recover. An open window is refreshing, but it also invites mosquitoes that can bite you at night, causing itching and potentially transmitting diseases. Closing the window traps heat and stale air. A screen solves this dilemma, allowing you to sleep with the window open, enjoying cool breezes and fresh air without the intrusion of insects. Screens also keep out dust and pollen, beneficial for allergy sufferers.

Best Screen Types for Bedroom Windows

For bedrooms, aesthetics and quiet operation are important. We recommend:

  • Fixed Frame Screens – The most common and economical. A rigid aluminum frame holds the mesh. Available in any colour to match your window trim. Durable and provides a tight seal.
  • Retractable Roll‑Up Screens – For a minimalist look, retractable screens roll up into a slim cassette at the top of the window. When not in use, the screen disappears completely, leaving your view unobstructed. Ideal for picture windows or when you want a clean aesthetic.
  • Magnetic Screens – For renters or those who want a no‑drill option, magnetic screens attach with tape. They are easy to remove for cleaning but may not be as durable for daily use.

Mesh Material: PET vs. 304 Stainless Steel for Bedrooms

For bedrooms, PET fiber mesh (18×16) is the preferred choice because it offers the highest transparency (85% openness). You’ll barely notice it, preserving your view of the outdoors. PET is lightweight, UV‑resistant, and rust‑proof. It lasts 5‑10 years. For homes with pets that may scratch the screen, choose 304 stainless steel mesh – it’s pet‑proof and lasts longer, but is slightly less transparent (75% openness). Both are available in grey, dark grey, or black. Dark grey mesh actually reduces glare and improves outward visibility during the day.

How to Measure for a Bedroom Window Screen

  • Measure the inside width and height of the window opening at three points each (top, middle, bottom; left, center, right). Use the smallest measurement.
  • For windows that open outward, measure the outside frame dimensions.
  • Subtract 2‑3mm from each dimension for a fixed frame screen to allow easy installation.
  • If your bedroom window is near a bed, consider a screen that can be easily removed for cleaning – Z‑frames with spring clips are great for this.

DIY Installation – Simple and Quick

Installing a bedroom window screen is a weekend task. For fixed frames: clean the window frame, place the screen into the opening, and secure with spring clips or screws (pre‑drill holes). For retractable screens: mount the cassette above the window, attach the side guides, and pull the screen down. For magnetic screens: just clean the frame and apply the adhesive magnetic tape. All hardware is included. Basic tools like a drill and screwdriver may be needed for fixed or retractable screens.

Noise Reduction and Light Transmission

Screens do not significantly reduce noise or light. Our fine mesh allows sound and light to pass through almost unaffected. In fact, by allowing you to keep windows open, screens can improve natural ventilation and reduce the need for air conditioning.

Child and Pet Safety

Our screens are designed with safety in mind. Fixed frame screens are securely attached and cannot be pushed out by a child. Retractable screens have no hanging cords – they use a spring mechanism that is safe for children. For homes with cats that scratch, choose stainless steel mesh to prevent tearing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will a screen block my view at night?
A: No. Our PET mesh has over 85% openness. You’ll see clearly both day and night.

Q: Can I install a screen on a bedroom window that opens outward?
A: Yes. Use an interior‑mounted fixed frame screen or a retractable screen that pulls down from the top.

Q: How do I clean my bedroom window screen?
A: Vacuum with a brush attachment. For deeper cleaning, remove the screen (if removable) and rinse with mild soap and water.

Q: What if my bedroom window is an unusual shape?
A: We manufacture custom‑shaped screens – arched, circular, triangular. Provide a template or detailed drawing.
