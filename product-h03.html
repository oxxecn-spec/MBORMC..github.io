Dual‑Action Protection: Insect‑Proof + Solar‑Shield

The MBORMC® H03 is more than just a mosquito screen – it is a thermal barrier that keeps your home cool while letting fresh air circulate. The high‑density PET fiber mesh is specially engineered to reflect a significant portion of solar radiation, reducing indoor temperatures during peak summer hours. This makes it an ideal solution for west‑facing doors, sunrooms, conservatories, and tropical climates where heat and insects are constant challenges.

Superior insect protection: The fine PET mesh blocks mosquitoes, flies, wasps, midges, and even butterflies, creating a comfortable bug‑free environment. Unlike traditional metal screens, PET fiber is lightweight, rust‑proof, and easy to clean – perfect for coastal or humid areas.

Retractable roll‑up mechanism: The H03 rolls up into a compact cassette at the side of the door when not needed, preserving your view and door aesthetics. It operates smoothly left‑right (single leaf) or both directions (bi‑parting for double doors). The spring‑loaded system ensures the screen stays taut and wrinkle‑free for years.

Installation requirements: Because the H03 is a precision‑engineered system, proper installation is critical. You will need a spirit level, drill, and basic measuring tools to mount the cassette and guide rails. While the process is documented with step‑by‑step instructions, a confident DIYer or local handyman is recommended for a perfect finish. We provide full technical support and video guides.

Factory direct & global shipping: Every H03 is custom‑manufactured at our MBORMC facility in Dali Town, Nanhai, Foshan, China based on your exact width and height measurements. After production, each unit is securely packed in a wooden crate and shipped via sea freight (or air freight for urgent orders) to destinations worldwide – including the UK, USA, Australia, Canada, and Europe.
